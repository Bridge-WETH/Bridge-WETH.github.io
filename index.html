What is Bridge WETH?

Across is a cross-chain transfer app for moving tokens between supported networks; WETH is wrapped ether, an ERC-20 representation of ETH explained by ethereum.org. Use this page as a clean route into the official Across app, then verify the network pair and token before signing.

Bridge WETH FAQ

How do I bridge weth with fewer mistakes?

Open the official Across app, confirm the domain is across.to, select WETH and the networks, then review the quote and wallet prompts before signing.

Is WETH the same as ETH when moving across networks?

No. WETH is an ERC-20 representation of ETH; native ETH is still needed for gas on many EVM networks.

Which WETH contract should I check?

For Ethereum mainnet, verify the canonical WETH contract on Etherscan; other networks may use different addresses.

Does Across show details before signing?

The app presents route and transaction details before wallet approval; network gas and quoted amounts can change until submitted.

Can I send WETH to Arbitrum or Base?

Across supports Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, and other networks in its app; choose only routes currently offered in the interface.
