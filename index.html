What is Bridge WETH?

Across is a cross-chain transfer app for moving tokens between supported networks; WETH is wrapped ether, an ERC-20 representation of ETH explained by ethereum.org. Use this page as a clean route into the official Across app, then verify the network pair and token before signing.

How to bridge WETH to Arbitrum Optimism Polygon or Base

To bridge WETH across EVM networks, choose a route that explicitly supports WETH as the input and verify the output token contract on the destination chain. Some bridges transfer WETH as a token; others unwrap, bridge native ETH, or route through liquidity. The safest pre-check is whether the destination app wants WETH or native ETH. For Ethereum mainnet, WETH is the canonical contract at 0xC02aaA39b223FE8D0A0e5C4F27eAD9083C756Cc2, which is listed on WETH.io and Etherscan.

WETH bridge fees and unwrap costs

WETH bridge costs can include token approval, bridge transaction gas, destination execution, and an unwrap transaction if you need native ETH after arrival. On Ethereum, wrapping and unwrapping are smart-contract interactions, so they cost gas. On L2s, costs are usually lower but still not zero. Users comparing bridge quotes should check whether the output is WETH or ETH, whether an approval is required, and whether the bridge fee is charged in the source token, destination gas token, or deducted from the received amount.

Can I use bridged WETH for gas?

Usually no: WETH is an ERC-20 token, while gas is paid with the chain's native gas asset. On Ethereum and many L2s that asset is ETH, but WETH itself is not the same balance as native ETH. You may need to unwrap WETH into ETH on the destination chain before using it for gas, if the chain and app support unwrapping. Keep a small native gas balance available before bridging WETH into a new network.

WETH token address on Ethereum

The canonical WETH token on Ethereum mainnet is 0xC02aaA39b223FE8D0A0e5C4F27eAD9083C756Cc2. Other networks have their own WETH or wrapped native ETH contracts, so do not copy the Ethereum address into another chain and assume it is correct. When bridging, use the destination explorer and the protocol's token list to confirm the WETH contract. This is especially important for lending, LP, and NFT marketplace workflows where a wrong token contract may not be accepted.

Bridge WETH FAQ

Can I unwrap WETH after bridging?

You can unwrap WETH only if the destination network has a supported WETH contract and interface for withdrawing native ETH. On Ethereum this is standard. On other networks, the exact wrapper contract and app support vary. Check the destination explorer and use a reputable interface before approving or unwrapping.

Why did I receive WETH instead of ETH?

Some bridge routes deliver the ERC-20 version because the input asset was WETH or the destination liquidity pool uses WETH. That does not necessarily mean the transfer failed. It means your balance is held as a token contract rather than native gas ETH. If you need gas, unwrap or bridge native ETH next time.

Is WETH always worth the same as ETH?

WETH is designed to be redeemable 1:1 for ETH through the wrapper contract on its network, but market prices can show tiny differences because of liquidity, chain, or routing. The bigger risk is usually not price parity; it is using the wrong WETH contract or a bridged representation that an app does not support.

Do I need approval to bridge WETH?

Most bridges require an ERC-20 approval before they can move WETH from your wallet. That approval is a separate transaction from the bridge transfer and costs gas. Use exact or limited approvals when possible, and revoke unused approvals later if you no longer need the bridge contract.

Can WETH be bridged to non-EVM chains?

WETH can exist on non-EVM chains through specialized wrapped or bridged assets, but those tokens are not the Ethereum ERC-20 contract. If you bridge to a non-EVM network, verify the issuer, bridge route, and destination liquidity carefully. A familiar ticker alone is not enough to confirm equivalence.

Should I bridge ETH or WETH to an L2?

Bridge ETH if your first need is gas and basic wallet funding. Bridge WETH if the destination protocol specifically requires WETH for trading, lending, LP positions, or NFT bids. Many users bridge a small amount of native ETH for gas first, then move WETH or swap once on the destination chain.
